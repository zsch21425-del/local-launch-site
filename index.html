How long does it take?

Most sites are live in under a week. The real work — SEO rankings, Google profile traction, review accumulation — builds over the first 60–90 days. I move fast on the build, then the system does the rest.

Do I need a contract?

No. Every tier is a one-time build. If you want ongoing maintenance (T2/T3), it's month-to-month with no lock-in. Cancel anytime. I earn your business every 30 days — you're never trapped.

What's included in monthly maintenance?

T2 ($35/mo) covers SEO upkeep, directory management, AI receptionist hosting, and strategy check-ins. T3 ($50/mo) adds the AI Visualizer, content publishing, Google Ads management, and competitor monitoring.

Can you help with Google Business Profile?

Yes — every tier includes GBP optimization. I audit your profile, fix categories, add service areas, and set up Google Posts. If you don't have one yet, I provide the step-by-step walkthrough to get verified. Once you're live, reviews and rankings compound fast.

What small businesses do you work with?

Plumbers, mechanics, landscapers, electricians, roofers, HVAC techs, painters, concrete contractors, pavers — any small business that serves local customers. I build the site around your specific services and the cities you work in.

How is this different from a marketing agency?

Agencies charge $2,000–$7,500/month with 6–12 month contracts. You're one of 50 accounts. I'm a solo operator in Greenville — you talk directly to me. The AI tools do the heavy lifting, so you get agency-level output at a fraction of the price.
